Welcome to La Passion.

We are a local family run hair salon with a loyal customer base and a great reputation. Our stylists are skilled at creating any style, for both male and female customers and we are always friendly and very welcoming. We work on an appointment basis, so simply either just give us a call, contact through our Facebook page, or pop in to the shop to book an appointment. We look forward to seeing you...

Meet the team

Kasia

Kasia has built up an enviable reputation after hairdressing for over 20 years. She is extremely driven, and prides herself on running a friendly and efficient Salon. She also manages the day-to-day business, working on innovating the Salon's practices to continue to improve the cuts she and Viola give.

Viola

Viola is a highly skilled and qualified stylist with over 14 years experience. If you want a stylish cut, Viola can accommodate anything you like.
